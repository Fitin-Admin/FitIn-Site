Member Care

Support

We are committed to a reliable, respectful support experience for every FitIn member.

Whether you need help regaining account access, reporting behavior, resolving a billing issue, or troubleshooting the app, our team is here to help quickly and clearly.

Contact

For all support matters, email support@fitin.fit.

Include your account email and a clear description of the issue so we can assist you faster.

Support Topics

Account access

Help with sign-in issues, verification flow problems, or account recovery steps.

Reporting users

Report harassment, impersonation, spam, or behavior that violates community rules.

Subscription issues

Questions about billing, renewals, cancellations, charge concerns, or receipts.

Technical help

Support for crashes, failed uploads, messaging glitches, or update-related issues.

Safety concerns

Escalated concerns that may involve immediate risk or sensitive trust matters.

Response Expectations

Our team reviews incoming requests in the order they are received, with priority for urgent safety reports and account integrity incidents.

  • Typical first response: within 1-3 business days
  • Urgent safety matters are prioritized for faster review
  • Complex issues may require additional follow-up before final resolution

Best Way to Get Help

To receive the fastest support, send one complete email with relevant details: your account email, device type, screenshots when available, and the approximate time the issue occurred. This helps our team verify context and reduce back-and-forth.
