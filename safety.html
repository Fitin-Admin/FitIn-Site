Child Safety Standards

FitIn is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and secure environment for all users. We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy for any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Age Requirement

FitIn is strictly for individuals aged 18 and older. We do not allow minors on the platform. Any accounts suspected of being underage will be removed immediately.

Prohibited Content

  • Any content involving minors in sexual or exploitative situations
  • Sexualized images or conversations involving individuals under 18
  • Attempts to engage minors through the platform

Reporting & Enforcement

Users can report inappropriate behavior directly within the app. Our moderation team reviews all reports and takes immediate action when violations are identified.

  • Immediate account suspension or permanent ban
  • Content removal
  • Reporting to law enforcement when required

Compliance

FitIn complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations and cooperates with law enforcement agencies when necessary.

Contact

If you have any concerns regarding child safety or CSAE-related issues, please contact us at:
support@fitin.fit

We are committed to ensuring a safe platform for all users and continuously improving our safety systems.
