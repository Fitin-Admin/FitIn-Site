Account Control

Delete Account

FitIn members may request deletion of their account and associated personal data, subject to legal and safety retention requirements.

We process deletion requests in a secure, verification-first workflow to protect members from unauthorized account actions.

How to Request Account Deletion

Email support@fitin.fit from, or referencing, the email tied to your FitIn account.

  • Recommended subject line: Delete My Account
  • Include your FitIn account email
  • Include any additional details that help us verify your request

What Happens After Your Request

After receiving your request, our team reviews and verifies ownership of the account. Once validated, we begin deletion of your profile and associated data within our standard processing timeline.

You may receive follow-up communication if additional details are needed before deletion can be completed.

Identity Verification

To prevent unauthorized deletion, FitIn may request verification information before processing an account deletion request. This may include confirming your account email or other information reasonably necessary to establish account ownership.

Retention Exceptions

Certain limited records may be retained after deletion when required for legitimate purposes, including fraud prevention, platform security, legal compliance, enforcement of terms, and dispute resolution.

Any retained data is restricted to the extent reasonably necessary for those purposes.

In-App Deletion Tools

If your version of the FitIn app includes in-app account deletion controls, you may use those tools as an alternative path. In-app deletion requests are handled under the same verification, safety, and legal retention framework described on this page.

Need Help?

If you have questions about deletion, data retention, or account access, contact support@fitin.fit.
