Account Deletion – Fit In

To request deletion of your account and associated data:

Option 1 (In-App)

  1. Open the Fit In app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"

Option 2 (Email Request)

Email: support@fitin.fit

Subject: Delete Account Request

Data Deleted

  • Profile information
  • Photos
  • Matches and messages
  • User preferences

Data Retention

Data is deleted within 30 days. Some data may be retained for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes.
